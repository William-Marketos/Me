The Legend

★

I am a materials science student driven by curiosity and a strong work ethic, with a genuine interest in understanding how the world works from the ground up. My studies have given me a deeper appreciation for the structures, properties, and processes that shape the materials around us, and I'm particularly drawn to applying that knowledge in practical, hands-on ways. I enjoy working with my hands—whether that's building, fixing, or experimenting—and I value the satisfaction that comes from turning ideas into something tangible.

"Those who do not return become a part of the clouds, a part of the animals that hide in the ravines and of the water that comes from the Earth. They become a part of everything... they become the Soul of the World"

Outside of academics, I'm most at home in the outdoors. I have a deep appreciation for nature and spend as much time as I can exploring it, whether that's through hiking, being around animals, or simply taking in the environment. This connection to the natural world fuels my curiosity and keeps me grounded, constantly reminding me of how much there is to learn and understand. I'm someone who enjoys learning broadly - about science, the environment, and the world at large - and I'm always looking for opportunities to grow, challenge myself, and expand my perspective.

William Marketos, c. unknown year

Schooling

★

The world is his classroom

I

KEPS and beyond (Ages 5-7, 8–14)

I went to King Edward VII Preparatory school in Johannesburg. A public school in Houghton.

II

The Outback (Ages 7-8)

This year was actually spent in the outback of Australia living out of a caravan with my family.

III

KES (Ages 13-18)

Highschool was a very enjoyable time. I played many sports – Rugby, Athletics, and Rowing, and I participated in First Aid for the School. I was a Prefect and got Team Colours for Rowing and First Aid, and I achieved full Academic Colours and finished 5th in the grade overall, achieving the top awards for Biology, History and Physics.

IV

UCT (Ages 18-22)

I have been at the University of Cape Town for almost 5 years now and have learnt a lot. I have done almost every Science Faculty course at UCT, and several from the Humanities and EBE faculties too. I love learning new and interesting things and this passion is what has lead me to taking so many electives, and also to changing my Major so many times in undergrad. I eventually settled on Chemistry and Computer Science and graduated with distinction in Chemistry and the degree with distiction. I am not in my Materials Science Honours course and am very much looking forward to what this year has in store for me.

Aspirations

Always living in the Moment. Always dreaming of the Future.

After my time on in the Bush I plan on moving to Australia temporarily and working on a Ranch there. I already have plans to learn how to ride a horse and motorcycle at my uncle's farm during the upcoming July break.

Hobbies & Passions

★

A life lived in full, unhurried colour

Doodling in his Journal

I have written down almost every day of my adult life in my Journal along with many of my musings and thoughts. I draw frequently in it as well, and really enjoy it. It is a nice escape and a way to express myself and my thoughts in a different way.

Exploring the World

I love travelling and plan to do many an adventure in my lifetime. I have already driven across Namibia and gone offroading in Botswana. I have family in Greece, Spain, Australia, Germany, Thailand and the UK and so have spent time in each of those countries visiting them. I love the excitement and adventure of it but to be honest South Africa is still my home and far and away my fanourite place to be.

Dopping

There is just nothing quite like a beer with a good friend to lighten up your day.

Inspiring Others

I have given many a speech in my time, from my time as a prefect in highschool to me being in the House Committee in my residence in University. I always love the opportunity to inspire others and share my thoughts and ideas with them, and I hope to do much more of it in the future.

Saving Lives

I love saving lives.

Adventuring

Never one to turn down an invitation I have done multi-day hikesin the Kruger National Park, spent many a cold night atop the Drakensburg, slept in my car in the middle of the Namib desert during a sandstorm, done GunRun in under 2 hours with an injured ankle, and done the Argus in plakkies and rugby broeke. I love a good adventure and am always looking for the next one.

Donating to those in Need

I am willing to donate almost every thing I have. I have donated blood, am a registered organ donor, have applied to be a stem-cell donor and have a kid.
